About me

Hello! I'm MANISH PATIL, a Data Analyst and Data Engineer based in Long Beach, CA. Proficient in SQL, Python, and various other data analytics and data engineering tools.

I specialize in transforming data into actionable insights.

Employing techniques such as Beautiful Soup and Selenium, I meticulously extracted precise financial data, contributing to the generation of 25,000 NLP annotations through the Doccano tool. This initiative significantly enhanced NLP models with enriched financial datasets, showcasing my expertise in data extraction, analytics, and NLP integration.

During coding sessions, I facilitated an interactive learning environment, breaking down intricate concepts to enhance students' proficiency and assisting them in overcoming coding challenges.

Outside of academic pursuits, my enthusiasm extends to photography (explore my 500px portfolio) and soccer. I take pride in my representation of the college team and active participation in MDFA, a district-level tournament in MUMBAI.
